Normal Trig Functions

The normal trig functions take the measure of an angle in a right triangle and gives out the ratio between two side lengths The basic functions are:

  • sine or sin
  • cosine or cos
  • tangent or tan

Sine is the ratio between the lrg opposite the angle and the hypotenuse. Cosine is the ratio between the leg adjacent to the angle and the hypotenuse. Tangent is the ratio between the leg opposite the angle and the leg adjecent to the angle.

Inverse Functions

The inverse trig functions take the ratio between two sides of a right and spits out the measure of the angle. The functions are:

  • cosecant or sin-1
  • secant or cos-1
  • arctangent or tan-1
